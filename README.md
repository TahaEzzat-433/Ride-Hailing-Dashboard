# Ride-Hailing Operations Analysis Dashboard

This dashboard provides a 360-degree view of ride-hailing operations, focusing on service efficiency, revenue streams, and customer satisfaction. It analyzes performance across 100K rides and 9,000 customers.

## Key Operational Metrics

**Performance Overview**: Tracking 100K total rides with a high Success Rate of 85.07%.

**Revenue Analysis**: Total revenue generated is $1.62M, with deep dives into seasonal and quarterly trends.

**Resource Management**: Monitoring a fleet of 900 drivers and a total of 38.74K ride hours.

## Deep Dive Insights

**Surge & Status Analysis**: Ability to filter data by Surge vs. Normal Fares and monitor ride outcomes (Completed, Canceled, or No-show).

**Time-Series Growth**: Visualization of the Number of Rides and Revenue by Year and Quarter, showing peak performance periods and growth trajectories.

**Customer Experience**: Analysis of Rides by Customer Rating, showing that 40.2% of rides received a 4-star rating, which helps in identifying service quality levels.

**Peak Demand Tracking**: Breakdown of ride volume by day of the week, identifying Thursday as a high-demand day with 15K rides.

## Technical Implementation

**Dynamic Filtering**: Integrated slicers for Date, Ride Status, and Surge types for an interactive user experience.

**Advanced Visuals**: Use of Gauge charts for success rates, Donut charts for rating distributions, and combined Bar/Line charts for revenue trends.

## How to use

1-Download the .pbix file.

2-Open it using Power BI Desktop.

3-Use the Slicers on the top/side of each page to filter data by Ride Status, Date, or Surge.
